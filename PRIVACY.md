# Privacy Policy — Followup

**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

## What Followup does

Followup helps companions organize their observations before a medical provider encounter. It asks questions, generates a timestamped summary, and saves records locally on your device.

## What data we collect

None.

- No accounts are created
- No data is transmitted from your device
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
- No network requests are made by the app (the app works entirely offline)

## Where your data is stored

All data — your answers, generated summaries, and saved records — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It never leaves your phone.

If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

## Microphone / speech recognition

If you choose to use voice input, audio is processed by your device's built-in speech recognition service (Google Speech Services on Android, Apple Speech on iOS). Followup does not record, store, or transmit audio. The microphone is only active while you hold the voice input button, and the resulting text is stored locally like any typed answer.

## Third-party services

Followup does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. There are no ads.

## Children's privacy

Followup does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
