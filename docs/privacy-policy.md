# Followup Privacy Policy

*Effective: July 6, 2026*

Followup is designed so that we cannot see your data, because there is nothing
for us to see.

## What we collect

Nothing. Followup has no accounts, no backend servers, no analytics, and no
advertising. We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any personal
information or health information.

## Where your records live

Everything you enter — names, observations, generated records — is stored only
in the app's local storage on your device. Deleting a record in the app, or
uninstalling the app, permanently removes that data. There is no copy anywhere
else and no way for us to recover it.

## Sharing

Records leave your device only when you explicitly choose to share them — by
copying to the clipboard, using your device's share sheet, or exporting a PDF.
Where a shared record goes (a text message, an email, a printer) is governed by
the apps you share it to, not by Followup.

## Voice input

If you use the optional microphone button, your device's built-in speech
recognition service converts your speech to text. Depending on your device and
settings, that service (provided by Apple or Google) may process audio on their
servers. Followup itself never receives or stores audio. If you prefer, type
instead — every question accepts typed input.

## Children

Followup is intended for adults (parents, caregivers, and companions). It does
not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Medical disclaimer

Followup is a documentation tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis,
or treatment. If you believe you are facing a life-threatening emergency, alert
medical staff or call 911.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will ship with the app and be
dated at the top. Because the app collects nothing, changes would only ever
describe new optional features.
